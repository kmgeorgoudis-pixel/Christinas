Our Story

The Beginning

Christina's journey began 25 years ago when my mother, Georgia, decided to open a small, sweet shop in a beautiful alley of Kokkari. Her talent in fashion and her incredible energy played a decisive role in the evolution of the business. Always a step ahead of the trends, she constantly sought new ideas and managed to thrive in the world of trade.

Expansion (2019)

In 2019, we moved to a new and larger location in the Kokkari square. This allowed space for all of Georgia's pioneering ideas—not only in clothing but in many other areas. It was a new chapter that brought us closer to the heart of Samos.

Mother & Daughter

Today, I, Christina, proudly follow in my mother's footsteps. Together and united, we put our best selves forward, following the path of evolution with positive energy. Our goal remains the best possible service for you, with love and style.

Thank you for being part of our history!
